You cannot donate blood if:

  • You had hepatitis B, hepatitis C, HIV/AIDS, or sexually transmitted infections.
  • You have chronic diseases like diabetes, heart disease, or cancer (unless cleared by a doctor).
  • You have taken antibiotics, steroids, or alcohol in the last 24–48 hours.
  • You’ve undergone major surgery or tattooing/piercing in the past 6 months.
  • You’re pregnant, breastfeeding, or recently gave birth (within 6 months).
  • You recently received a COVID-19 vaccine — wait 14 days before donating.
